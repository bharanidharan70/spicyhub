About Us

Spicy Hub is a family restaurant proudly serving authentic South and North Indian cuisine since 2015. We are committed to delivering rich flavors, traditional recipes, and unforgettable dining experiences.

Our dishes are prepared using fresh ingredients and time-honored cooking methods. With a warm ambience, hygienic kitchen practices, and friendly service, Spicy Hub is the perfect place for families, friends, and food lovers.

Whether you visit us for a quick meal or a special occasion, we strive to deliver consistent quality, exceptional service, and a truly satisfying dining experience every time.

Why Choose Us?

At Spicy Hub, we focus on delivering authentic taste with consistent quality. Every dish is prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to ensure rich flavors and satisfaction.

From a hygienic kitchen and experienced chefs to friendly service and affordable pricing, we prioritize customer happiness. Our warm ambience makes Spicy Hub the perfect place for family dining and celebrations.

We continuously strive to maintain high standards in taste, cleanliness, and service, ensuring that every visit to Spicy Hub leaves our customers happy and eager to return.



The founders, who hailed from a family with a rich tradition in the hotel industry, were deeply rooted in South Indian culture and cuisine. Inspired by their ancestors' culinary legacy, they set out to create an authentic dining experience that would transport diners to the vibrant streets of South India. They carefully curated recipes, ensuring that every dish embodied the essence and traditional Flavors of the region.

Beyond the culinary delights, the founders paid great attention to creating an ambiance that reflected the South Indian heritage. The restaurants were adorned with vibrant colors, intricate artwork, and traditional decor, instantly transporting guests to the enchanting landscapes of the region. The warm and friendly staff, dressed in traditional attire, further enhanced the dining experience, ensuring guests felt welcomed and cherished.
